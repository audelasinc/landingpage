The problem

Most travel products optimize for simple bookings. Event travel is the opposite. One delay can cascade into missed matches, lost lodging, and expensive last minute fixes across disconnected systems.

Fragmentation

Disconnected tools

Travelers stitch together itineraries manually, with no single view of how a flight delay affects the stadium entry window.

Execution risk

Cascading failures

Tight connections and city changes amplify disruption. Currently, recovery is 100% DIY and stressful.

Static Plans

No feedback loops

When schedules shift, you need a system that recalculates buffers and alternatives instantly, not just an email alert.

The solution

Voyavent operates at the application layer. The event is the anchor for the entire trip. We help orchestrate flights and ground transport around event constraints.

Anchor first

Event constrained planning

Build a trip that starts from kickoff time. We compute the arrival window, buffers, transfers, and fallback options automatically.

  • Arrival window logic Arrive with enough time for airport delays, city transit, and stadium entry.
  • Connection risk scoring Flag itineraries that look cheap but are time-fragile.
Monitor and adapt

Continuous trip health

Track schedule changes and disruption signals, then propose actionable adjustments while keeping the traveler in control.

  • Change detection Detect flight schedule shifts and knock-on effects immediately.
  • Contingency playbooks One-click booking for backup flights or earlier transfers.
